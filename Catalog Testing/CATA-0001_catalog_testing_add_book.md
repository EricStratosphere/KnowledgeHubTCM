## **CATA-0001:** Catalog testing - Add Book (Admin)

> **Summary:** Verify that the book is added and displayed in the online catalog successfully.  <br>

**Preconditions:** _User must be authenticated and an admin._  

Scenario 1 

 | \# | Step | Expected Behavior | 
 |----|------|-------------------| 
 |  1 | Launch application.                                 | Verify that the home screen is shown. | 
 |  2 | In the main page, click the plus icon on the upper right corner of the page.              | Verify that the user is redirected to the admin-page. |  
 |  3 | In the form, fill out the necessary fields, `Title, Author, Genre, Description, Cover Image, and E-Book PDF` afterwards click `Create Book`.              | Verify that the user has successfully added the book to the catalog of books. |  
 
**Post-conditions:**  

 _None_
